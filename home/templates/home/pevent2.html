    Name : The price is right

    Mode : Offline

    Description:

    We will ask them the cost of common everyday products that they use.
    Guidelines

    1. No phone !
    2. Initially, we will split you into two teams, one half this side
    3. You must guess the price !! - Only after I say go !
    4. Loudest shouts of the price will only be received
    5. Rapid elimination - We will keep dividing you into smaller teams, until you’re down to two teams.

    Tips to remember :

    Be energetic on stage, so that it transfers to the crowd.
    Values that members take back from the game :

    4. Time Management
    5. Team Work
    6. Market Research
    7. Dynamic Environment
    8. Adaptability

    Why is it easy to conduct?

    All you have to do is prepare questions that resonate with your crowd, & ask them.

    How we did it:

    The questions we asked :

    1. Rice - Free - Development is when food made free to all!
    2. Exam paper thread - 4 paisa per thread - An insignificant piece, without which there is no trust - 40 million people working in the cotton industry
    3. IIITDM K Actual ID card price - 10 - Logo value
    4. Internet Handling charges - 33 Rs per ticket - Theatres are supposed to pay to BookMyShow : Court case is in proceedings
    5. Plate - 150 - “It never goes out of style” - Rajini
    6. Jockey - Marketing - 200 - EPIC or nothing
    7. Local auto to Vandalur Zoo - Price Value - 250 - Baasha
    8. JIO fibre - 700 to 10,000 - Offers can attract anybody
    9. Cot - 4000 - A strong base
    10. IIIT admission fee - Company Maturity - 5000 - Segmentation of fee to earn more
    11. (Eruvamaten) - 10500 - 200ml - Marketing + Insecurity = Money
    12. Pewdiepie (Per month salary) - 6,80,00,000 - Influencer
    13. Blackberry - 25000 crores to -42000 crores Rs - If you don’t catch up, you’ll fail - Change is inevitable
