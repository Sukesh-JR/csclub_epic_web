    Name : Team Rush

    Mode : Offline

    Description:

    Itâ€™s a memory exercise that shows how well a team co-ordinates amongst itself.
    Guidelines

    1. Split the crowd into 4 teams
    2. Pick four categories, e.g. : Category A - Fruits, Category B - Veggies, etc.
    3. Provide one item out of each category to each person, e.g. 1st person - mango -> goes to Team A
    4. Make sure only that person knows his/her item.
    5. Ask all fruits to form a team, all veggies to form a team, etc.
    6. Assign a captain to each team. Now the first captain, after consulting with their team, have to call out one item from any other category. If there is a person associated with that item, they move from their team to the winning team.
    7. At the end of a fixed duration, the team with the max no. of people win.

    Tips to remember :

    Be calm, this game can get really confusing. Make sure everybody plays by the rules.
    Values that members take back from the game :

    1. Team Work
    2. Memory power
    3. Co-ordination

    Why is it easy to conduct?

    All you have to do is prepare 4 categories, assign them & the game plays out on its own
