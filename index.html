Welcome to EPiC

About Us

We are a student-run club that provides a platform for entrepreneurship, networking & investment among engineering students. We create & conduct events that start & sustain meaningful discussions, & create awareness about “risky ventures” like entrepreneurship & investments.

Our Vision

To instill the values of entrepreneurship in students, & provide an environment that supports budding entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

To help student members to help themselves & create problem solvers a.k.a entrepreneurs out of them
