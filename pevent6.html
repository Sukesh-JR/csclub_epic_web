    Name : Model United Nations

    Mode : Offline

    Description:

    You will have to conduct a united nations general assembly & mediate the conversation. Better explained online

    Tips to remember :

    Make sure you are updated on the topic of discussion & mediate without bias. It would also be more fun, if you conduct it how an actual MUN is conducted.
    Values that members take back from the game :

    1. Power of delegation
    2. Research
    3. Public speaking skills

    General points :

    Do try to theme your events around present trends / social causes to attract more sponsors, loyalty from your audience & to feel a greater sense of purpose.
